Muesli Recipe

Description

Muesli is a mix of oats, dried fruit, seeds, and nuts. It is an equally filling, healthy, and delicious breakfast!

This cereal tastes great with milk, yogurt, and even water.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup wheat bran
  • 1 cup rasins
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine oats, wheat bran, dried fruit, nuts, and seeds.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Store muesli in an airtight container.
  4. When ready to consume, pour enough yogurt to fully cover the cereal.
  5. Stir well, and let the mixture sit for 10 minutes until oats get soft.
  6. Enjoy!
