Omlette Recipe

Description

Not only is an omelette quick and easy to make, it is a paragon of economy. Any leftovers rise to a new level when placed inside an omelette.

Leftover, cooked vegetables paired with a little cheese and folded into eggs present a surprisingly cheerful meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheese
  • 3 cherry tomatoes, cut in half
  • 2 tablespoons chopped basil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with a fork.
  2. In a nonstick skillet over medium-low heat, melt the butter.
  3. Add the eggs to the skillet and cook without stirring for about 6 minutes.
  4. Place the cheese, tomatoes, and herbs onto the omlette and cook for 1 minute longer.
  5. Fold the omlette in half and put it onto the plate!
  6. Enjoy!
