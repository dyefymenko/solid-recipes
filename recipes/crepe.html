Crepe Recipe

Description

Breakfast crepes are an easy, yet elegant meal. They are a good addition to your morning cup of coffee or tea, they taste good with juice, and are just as great on their own!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine flour and sugar.
  2. Whisk in the eggs and milk until smooth, stirring constantly.
  3. Whisk in the melted butter.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat, brush it with a little butter.
  5. For each crepe, pour 3 tbsp of batter in the center of the skillet. Cook for about 30 second on each side.
  6. Plae the cooked crepes on a plate as you go
  7. Enjoy!
